## Advantages & Disadvantages & Agree & Disagree

* ### 框架
	1. Nowadays, ... . From my perspective, while A  causes ... to some extent, the positive effects of this trend **far outweigh** the disadvantages. / This essay will explore the merits of ... , as well as the arguments supporting ... . 
	2. For individuals / To begin with, ... (好处/坏处1，==举具体的例子、细节==)
	3. For businesses, ... (好处/坏处2，==举具体的例子、细节==)
	4. Despite these advantages / Admittedly, ... . A more practical / reasonable solution would be to ... .(让步、 反驳， 给出应对方案 )
	5. In conclusion, although ... , the benefits to ... are more substantial. A balanced policy that ... would be ... ./ I believe it is less convincing, since ... . Therefore / On balance, ... remains more reasonable. (==重申观点==)

* ### 用句积累
	* Such benefits are less likely to be achieved when ...
	* Therefore, its negative effects are serious but not necessarily permanent.

* ### 用词积累
	* 导致 lead to / result in / cause / 
	* 重大的/明显的 substantial / significant / evident
	* 就...而言 when it comes to / in terms of  / regarding ...
	* 起重要作用 play a pivotal role / it is necessary to ... 
	* 缺点 disadvantage / drawback

### 1. Machines

Nowadays, machines, ~~especcially~~**especially** robots, ~~gradually take~~**are increasingly taking** ==**the**== place of human labor in jobs and tasks which ~~need~~**involve** hard physical work. From my perspective, while such ~~movement~~**trend** ~~does~~ causes some ~~problems about employment~~**employment problems** to some extent, the positive effects of this trend far outweigh the disadvantages.

For individuals, hard physical work ~~always~~**can** ~~brings out~~**cause** irreversible ~~body damage~~**physical injuries**, which ~~will accompany~~**may affect** workers ~~during~~**for** the rest of their ~~life~~**lives**. It is beneficial ~~for~~**to workers'** health and safety to use machines to perform those difficult or dangerous tasks. **(可以举例子，化工厂、矿井等危险环境)** **For example, robots can operate in mines, construction sites, and factories where employees might otherwise be exposed to toxic substances, heavy loads, or serious accidents**

For businesses, machines have various advantages in industry ~~without any doubt~~. ~~Depending on~~**With** sophisticated programming, machines ~~have high pricision~~**can operate with high precision** and they can work continuously ~~for 7 days and 24 hours~~**24 hours a day, seven days a week**, which will increase ~~working efficiency~~**productivity** significantly and reduce the expenditure on labor. Furthermore, it is convenient to update ~~the machines~~**aotumated systems**, ~~apply~~**adopt** new technology, and expand ~~productivity~~**production capacity** in an automated industry. **As a result, businesses can manufacture products more efficiently while maintaining consistent quality**

Admittedly, ~~the movement of fully automated industry~~**the transition towards automation** will ~~decrease the available workplace~~**reduce the number of available jobs** and cause a wave of unemployment. Also there will be more ~~and more contenders~~**job seekers** competing for a ~~job~~**limited number of positions**. However, this tendency is ~~overwhelming~~**inevitable** and technological progress is bound to change the labor market. In order to be competitive ~~with machines~~**in the labor market**, it is ~~neccesary~~**necessary** for people to master ~~high-level~~**advanced** skills to manipulate machines and design the gaming rules. **(自动化不可阻挡并不能说明优点大于缺点，更有效的反驳：可与通过职业培训得到缓解，且自动化会创造编程、维修等新岗位)** **Moreover, automation can create new employment opportunities in areas such as programming, engineering, and equipment maintenance. Therefore, its negative effects are serious but not necessarily permanent**

In conclusion, although machines may ~~result in~~**lead to** more unemployment, their benefits are ~~large and conspicuous~~**substantial and evident**. The development of machines is reshaping labor market, which is ~~irresistable~~**irristible**. **With appropriate retraining programs, society can reduce job displacement while continuing to benefit from automation**

* 语法
	* always过于绝对，并非所有体力劳动都一定造成不可逆伤害。can更客观
	* movement常指社会运动或身体移动，不适合用于“趋势”
	* bring out表示某种品质显现出来，不能表示造成伤害
	* be beneficial ==to== sth
	* without no doubt比较口语化，雅思写作中最好直接陈述观点，不需要加强语气
	* depend on 取决于，with更适合“凭借”
	* 007: 24 hours a day, seven days a week
	* contender通常指比赛、竞选中的有力竞争者，不适合泛指求职者
	* overwhelming 数量极大的、令人难以承受的、压倒性的
	* conspicuous 显眼的，引人注目的，不是明显的
* 积累
	* 扩大产能 expand production capacity
	* 采用技术 adopt technology
	* 自动化 the transition towards automation
	* 求职者 job seeker
	* 在一些部门 in some sectors
	* 就业岗位 employment opportunities
	* 岗位替代 job displacement
